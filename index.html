About

Built by Simmers, for Simmers

"Say parking," a phrase every sim pilot dreads to hear. Let's be honest: it's a pain to check where every one of the 200 different airlines you fly for parks. Where do I park aims to solve that issue, so the next time you land at JFK, you'll know exactly which terminal and gate you'll be parking at.
